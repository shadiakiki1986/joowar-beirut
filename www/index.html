Joowar Beirut is a site where you can drive around areas in Beirut that I've drawn.

Instructions
  • Driving wrecklessly and hitting bumps and potholes will damage your car
  • Use the arrow keys to drive
  • Uphills and downhills are also modeled

  • La Madonna, Ashrafieh, ver 2: Drive, G Maps
